THE KILLSCREEN PROTOCOL

[ HOUSE RULES & CODE OF CONDUCT ]

Killscreen is for everyone. Whether you're here for the games, the cocktails, the food, or just a good night out. These rules keep it that way for all of us.

PLAYER STANDARDS // FOR THE GAMERS

  • 01. Zero-Toxic Tolerance: Trash talk is part of the game; harassment is not. Slurs, targeted abuse, or "raging" at other patrons will result in an immediate session termination. No warnings.
  • 02. Respect the Hardware: You are playing on top-of-the-line gear. Physical abuse of peripherals (slamming mice, hitting monitors) won't be tolerated. You break it, you buy it.
  • 03. The "GG" Rule: Respect the staff and your opponents. We are a community, not a ranked solo-queue lobby. Win with humility, lose with grace.

SOCIAL STANDARDS // FOR THE NON-GAMERS

  • 01. Respect the Flow: Don't interfere with active matches or distract players in high-stakes zones. Treat a gaming bay like you'd treat a pool table or a bar game in a traditional venue.
  • 02. Ask Before Casting: Killscreen is a highly aesthetic venue. While we encourage photos, please respect the privacy of gamers who may not want to be in your social media feed.
  • 03. Inclusivity is Mandatory: This is not a "boys' club." Killscreen is a safe space for women, LGBTQ+ individuals, and newcomers. If you make someone feel unwelcome, you'll be asked to leave.

ENFORCEMENT

Our staff have the final say. They can de-escalate situations, end gaming sessions, or ask anyone to leave in order to protect the space. Everyone deserves to have a good time here. Those who make that impossible for others won't be welcome back.
