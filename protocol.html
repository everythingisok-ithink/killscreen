THE KILLSCREEN PROTOCOL

[ OPERATIONAL STANDARDS & CODE OF CONDUCT ]

Killscreen is a dual-purpose environment. Whether you are here to climb the ladder or just grab a high-end cocktail, the following rules ensure the "Node" remains functional for everyone.

PLAYER STANDARDS // FOR THE GAMERS

  • 01. Zero-Toxic Tolerance: Trash talk is part of the game; harassment is not. Slurs, targeted abuse, or "raging" at other patrons will result in an immediate session termination. No warnings.
  • 02. Hardware Stewardship: You are playing on elite-tier kits. Physical abuse of peripherals (slamming mice, hitting monitors) is a breach of conduct. You break it, you buy it.
  • 03. The "GG" Rule: Respect the staff and your opponents. We are a community, not a ranked solo-queue lobby. Win with humility, lose with grace.

SOCIAL STANDARDS // FOR THE NON-GAMERS

  • 01. Respect the Flow: Don't interfere with active matches or distract players in high-stakes zones. Treat a gaming bay like you’d treat a pool table or a dart board in a traditional bar.
  • 02. Ask Before Casting: Killscreen is a highly aesthetic venue. While we encourage photos, please respect the privacy of gamers who may not want to be in your social media feed.
  • 03. Inclusivity is Mandatory: This is not a "boys' club." Killscreen is a safe space for women, LGBTQ+ individuals, and newcomers. If you make someone feel unwelcome, you’ll be asked to leave.

ADMINISTRATION & ENFORCEMENT

Our staff are the "Admins" of this space. They have the authority to de-escalate situations, revoke gaming access, or remove patrons to protect the environment. As a Veteran-owned business, we prioritize safety and order.
